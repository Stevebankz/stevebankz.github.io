Research Interests

Explainable & Robust AI

Layer-wise interpretability (e.g., LRP), causal rationale stability, and evaluation of deep models under adversarial or environmental perturbations.

Neuro-Symbolic & Causal Reasoning

Bridging neural representations with symbolic logic and causal abstractions to verify and align model reasoning with its actions.

NLP & Social Harm Detection

Weakly supervised and domain-adaptive NLP for misinformation, hate speech, and social harm, with emphasis on fairness and interpretability.

Multimodal & Vision Interpretability

Extending XAI methods from text to vision and multimodal systems for real-world monitoring, environmental and welfare applications.
